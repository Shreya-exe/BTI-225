Newnham Builings

Seneca College Newnham branch has so many buildings with different sector divided;

Building Name Departments/Sector Brief Details
A Building Computing Commons This location offers ample room for studying, working, or creating in a studio. Additionally, there are a small number of classrooms available. The facility includes a SandBox, which is a studio exclusively for Seneca College students. This studio has professional-grade audio and video recording equipment to meet industry standards.
K Building School Of Engineering This building houses various IT and Electronics labs primarily utilized by Engineering students. Additionally, classes for CSE, BSD, CNS, CNT, and other related subjects are held here.
G Building Sports and Gymnesium This facility includes a gym, an indoor playground with space for changing clothes, and various games, including basketball, badminton, cricket, and chess.
